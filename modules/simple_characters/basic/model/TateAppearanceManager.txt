PLANNED FEATURE
	TateAppearanceManager

What is it?
	Realtime modular armor, clothes, and accessories on characters.

How's it work?
	Bone attachments, scaling/inflating armor & accessories, and "paper dolling"
	parts of the original mesh away. 
	
	The characters are split up into every limb being its own mesh, then these meshes
	are dynamically hidden. In order to deal with the number of draw calls this adds,
	the meshes are baked together into a single mesh.
